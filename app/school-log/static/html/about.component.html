School Log

Welcome to the test prototype of the School Log application.

Under the Students menu, you can add, edit, and delete students. When creating a new student, please ensure that the name is spelled correctly. Entries in the database are indexed in part by student names, and therefore student names cannot be edited after creating them.

Under the Entries menu, you can view and filter your school activity entries as well as add new ones. Any blanks or zeroes in the query form will be ignored. Use this form to filter down to only those entries you want to view. The menu to add a new entry is at the bottom of the page, but a link is provided at the top to help you get to it if you have a lot of entries.

Feel free to submit an issue or feedback at the GitHub Repository or by e-mailing schoollog@johnfmarion.com .
